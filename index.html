The Mission of UXSEA Society is to build and add value to the people of SouthEast Asia region in an inclusive manner. SouthEast Asia is diverse, young, vibrant and different in some aspects than from other geographies like the North America, UK and the EU. The awareness about User Experience is still growing here. People in SEA typically have larger income gaps, are more Visual and love multimedia.

The UXSEA Society organizes yearly UX events in different countries across SouthEast Asia to help strengthen User Experience awareness and practice across Businesses, UX professionals and Academia in countries of South East Asia.
